About Us

Your Vision, Our Expertise

Welcome to our architectural haven in beautiful Northland, New Zealand! We put you, our client, at the heart of everything we do, taking on projects in Northland and throughout New Zealand.

Who We Are

Established fresh in 2024, our company is built on a solid foundation of extensive experience and a passion for design. Since 2012, our director has called New Zealand home, bringing with her a wealth of experience from a variety of projects across Europe and New Zealand. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for innovative design, she has tackled projects of all scales and types - from cozy single-unit homes to expansive commercial spaces of >20,000sqm across 8 levels.

Our journey is rooted in a deep commitment to client satisfaction and quality craftsmanship. 

We blend our global experience with a strong local presence, ensuring every project resonates with the unique character of its surroundings. Through our established associations with other firms, we seamlessly integrate additional expertise and resources as needed, delivering the best possible results for our clients.

We are proud to show, on demand, a portfolio of works where our director served as a Project Architect under other firms. These medium and large-scale endeavors each come with their own fascinating stories. Contact us today to find out more about these prior works and how we can bring our expertise to your project!

Meet Katerina
Founder and Director
MArch(Prof), NZRAB New Zealand Registered Architect

Normally, of course, I take the risk on construction site seriously! Here’s an untypical behind-the-scenes look at our home renovation in 2023, where I wore multiple hats as the architect, mother, and owner. While managing our construction site, I embraced the chaos with gusto – don't worry, my PPE is just out of shot! Our kids grew up in this dynamic environment, and they now have a keen sense of how to wield a hammer and screwdriver. 

As an architect, I love getting hands-on and diving into the nitty-gritty details. There's nothing like being on-site, collaborating with tradies, and brainstorming ideas with clients in real-time. It's all part of the creative journey, and I wouldn't have it any other way!
