What We Offer

We believe in the authenticity of places and people. Utilizing local resources, sustainable solutions, and smart technologies, we craft spaces that are as unique as the individuals we design for. No two projects are the same, because no two clients are the same. Let’s work together to turn your vision into reality.

 
If you are a developer or government client, go to the Medium- and Large-Scale Projects section. If you are building for personal use (such as a private home or business owner) and have limited experience with the construction process, scroll down to the Small-Scale Projects section.

Medium- and Large-Scale Projects

Our partnerships allow us to pool resources and expertise, providing you with the best value and highest quality results.

Working alongside planners and other consultants from early stage helps to speed up planning process and utilise your building side.

With extensive experience in feasibility studies for residential and commercial jobs, we leverage AI tools to test, optimize, and develop innovative solutions.

We have an excellent track record of working with a wide range of stakeholders, engaging in co-design processes to ensure all voices are heard.

We collaborate with local consultants for cultural articulation, ensuring the authenticity of place and people.

We are well-versed in projects targeting environmental certifications such as Green Star, Home Star, and ILFI, making our work suitable for both government initiatives and rentable spaces.

Integrating biophilic design in built environment, which we believe is essential and natural, helps elevate the spirit, and connect people with nature.

We welcome early contractor engagement and/or quantity surveyor appointment to discuss priorities, cost, construction methodology, supply chain challenges and phasing.

We understand tight timeframes and are able to deliver design split in phases for building consent processing and construction.

Small-Scale Projects

We want to understand your needs, values, and personality. Embarking on a building project can be one of the most significant and stressful investments you’ll ever make, especially for new homeowners. Whether you're building a new house, upgrading your current one, or creating a space for your business, we are here to help to make the process as smooth and stress-free as possible.

We guide you through the entire process—from design brief and sketches to working drawings, securing resource and building consents, and successful project completion. Our creativity ensures each project uniquely reflects your vision and lifestyle, providing a space that feels like your own and meets your budget expectations.

We align with your builder and offer building contract administration and observation services to ensure your dream is turned into reality.

Construction projects typically have seven stages, as outlined on the NZIA website. We will guide you through each step and create a tailored solution together.

Meet us for coffee to discuss how we can help!
