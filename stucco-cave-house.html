Stucco Cave House

Step into a world where ancient inspirations meet modern simplicity. This unique home, inspired by the client's travels through Europe and Africa, stands as a testament to solid structures and timeless elegance. Crafted with stone, textured plaster, brick, and rammed earth, the house embodies a sense of permanence and serenity.

The heart of this design lies in its dual spine walls, creating an inviting "cave" effect. These walls, finished with a textured plaster that echoes the look of rammed earth and sedimentary rock, bring the essence of geological history indoors. Arched openings gracefully connect the various spaces, ensuring privacy while maintaining an open, airy feel.

Beyond their aesthetic appeal, these walls function as thermal masses, regulating the interior temperature for year-round comfort. This ingenious design extends outdoors, where similar plaster techniques are applied to the wing walls. These external walls not only support the expansive roof overhang but also shield the ground floor living and dining areas from the prying eyes of a busy road junction, creating a private oasis for the homeowners.
