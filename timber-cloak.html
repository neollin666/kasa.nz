Timber Skin Holiday House

This charming two-bedroom holiday house, nestled in nature, reflects a love for natural materials and layered design. The home features two intersecting box volumes, fully clad in timber that will age to a silvery hue, blending seamlessly with the surrounding bushland. The timber cladding extends over the roof, camouflaging the house into its environment.

A distinctive tinted timber entry contrasts with the main structure, inviting you inside. The interior showcases a mix of timber finishes, natural-patterned tiles, playful terrazzo in the bathroom, and obscure glass in the shower for admiring the vast landscape in privacy.

Humble yet luxurious, stylish yet fun — a perfect retreat that combines quality and thoughtful design, celebrating the beauty of nature and craftsmanship.
