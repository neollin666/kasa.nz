Medium- and Large-Scale Projects

Our partnerships allow us to pool resources and expertise, providing you with the best value and highest quality results.

Working alongside planners and other consultants from early stage helps to speed up planning process and utilise your building side.

With extensive experience in feasibility studies for residential and commercial jobs, we leverage AI tools to test, optimize, and develop innovative solutions.

We have an excellent track record of working with a wide range of stakeholders, engaging in co-design processes to ensure all voices are heard.

We collaborate with local consultants for cultural articulation, ensuring the authenticity of place and people.

We are well-versed in projects targeting environmental certifications such as Green Star, Home Star, and ILFI, making our work suitable for both government initiatives and rentable spaces.

Integrating biophilic design in built environment, which we believe is essential and natural, helps elevate the spirit, and connect people with nature.

We welcome early contractor engagement and/or quantity surveyor appointment to discuss priorities, cost, construction methodology, supply chain challenges and phasing.

We understand tight timeframes and are able to deliver design split in phases for building consent processing and construction.
